The Principal Chain Doctrine

Legislative Reform Points

1. Unified Standing

Treat the entire delegation chain as co-defendants. Joint and several liability ensures recovery without identifying the micro-error.

2. Presumption of Linkage

Causation is presumed to flow through the delegator (analogous to s.113B Indian Evidence Act). Rebuttable only if agent acts outside scope.

Impact Model

  • REMEDY

    Bypasses the Causal Fog that currently leads to zero-recovery outcomes in Indian and EU courts.

  • SAFETY

    Forces developers to implement Handshake Guardrails (budget limits, etc.) to avoid shared liability.

  • MARKET

    Provides a consistent framework for cross-border agentic interactions, harmonizing international tort.
